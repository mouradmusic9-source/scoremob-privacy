Privacy Policy for Scoremob

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Scoremob ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Information We Collect

Scoremob does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. The app does not require account creation or login.

  • Local storage: The app may store preferences (such as favorite teams or app settings) directly on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Match data: The app fetches publicly available football match data from a third-party API (football-data.org) to display scores, fixtures, and standings. No personal data is sent as part of these requests.

Advertising

Scoremob may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services, which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob - Privacy Policy
  • football-data.org API - Website

Children's Privacy

Scoremob is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
